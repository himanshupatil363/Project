CAMPUS

The school is situated at a prime location in Parel, Mumbai surrounded by a child friendly environment. The main entrance on east has a memorial of the founder of the school, Late Shri. R. K. Shirodkar which reminds us that Dr. R.K. Shirodkar laid the foundation of the mass movement for providing education to the children of poor families. He was the first person to create a strong ideological and social movement for the total independence of mill laborers working in Mumbai. Beyond memorial lies our grand auditorium which has been an origin to many visionary students to make their dreams come true and also to create a memorable childhood experience which is cherished by us all throughout our life. Various momentous events held under these roofs are morning assemblies, cultural activities and competitions such as drama, dance, singing, elocution, performances, Science, Arts, Maths Exhibitions, Annual Functions, Teacher’s Day, Children’s Day event etc. Our school is a Centre for learning good habits at a tender age which has an everlasting impact. School campus hosts a wide variety of facilities to create an environment where the students can shape their future. We have various spaces for conferences, special events, receptions, and academic competitions. We give utmost importance to our facilities and learning resources and continuously ensure that our students have full access to everything they need to help them succeed in their field of study.

SECURITY

The school looks out for the safety and security of the children entailing fire safety measures involving 50 fire extinguisher and fire exits in the building. The structural form consists of 5 Floors which are well secured by 105 surveillance cameras throughout the building as well as trained professional security guards on all the main entrances and exits.

TRANSPORT FACILITY

‘Safety of the child is our first priority.’ The school also provides safe and comfortable transportation facilities to the students by school buses and school vans on a daily basis for bringing in students from different localities to the school and vice versa. We ensure that students are brought to school and dropped back home safely under our vigilance. Parents are kept well-informed of the routes and timings.

MUSIC ROOM

Music is given supreme importance as a part of the school curriculum. School has well-equipped music room which provides space for vocal and instrumental music classes. All basic musical equipments are made available to the students. Instruments like the harmonium, tabla, piano, keyboard and others are accessible for the students.

MATHEMATICS LABORATORY

Taking into consideration the recommendations of the National Curriculum Framework (NCF) developed by NCERT, school has designed Mathematics laboratory which is a place rich in adequate equipments to which children have ready access to handle them, perform mathematical experiments, play mathematical games, solve mathematical puzzles and become involved in other activities. The laboratory approach allows pupil to set up mathematical experiment for the purpose of discovering some mathematical principles, patterns or process. Mathematics laboratory plays vital role in exploring mathematical concepts to learn, to stimulate interest and to develop favorable attitudes towards mathematics.

SCIENCE LABORATORY

School has well-equipped laboratories for imparting practical knowledge in Physics, Chemistry and Biology. The aim of the Science lab is to develop scientific reasoning abilities as well as practical skills and to increase understanding of the nature of Science. Laboratory is well-stocked with sufficient chemicals, specimens and adequate equipments so that every student gets the opportunity to carry out the experiments individually as per the CBSE guidelines.

COMPUTER LABORATORY

The school integrates Information and Communication technology into the curriculum and thus provides air conditioned, highly modernized computer lab with the latest technology and high speed internet access. Students are taught various computer programs according to the demands of the modern times. The hi-tech computer lab make the students tech-savvy and keeps them updated with the latest techniques & that teaches not just computer as a subject, but also, the integration of computer skills for learning of other subjects in the curriculum. Our computer lab is accessible to the students during and after school hours.

AUDIO-VISUAL ROOM

Our school has a fully equipped Audio Visual Room which simplifies learning through multi-media facilities. Our Audio Visual Room is a learning facility meant to enhance the interest in knowledge and to foster interaction between students and teachers. The Audio-Visual room provides important aids for language learning and socio-linguistic skills. Students watch animated videos and improve their listening, speaking, reading and writing skills.
